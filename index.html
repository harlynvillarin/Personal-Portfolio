About

Hi! I'm Harlyn, a Filipino front-end developer and email copywriter based in the Philippines.

I began my career in 2013 as a customer service representative for a US Healthcare Insurance Account, where I honed my customer service skills. It was not an easy task. As the front liner, you must be professional, patient, and have a "people-first" attitude. Working with clients, on the other hand, has taught me patience and perseverance over the years.

Five years later, I began working as a machine operator in Taiwan for a Taiwanese Integrated Circuit Company. It is difficult to learn to operate a machine, test an integrated circuit, and collaborate with coworkers who speak a different language, but grit and determination have been my weapons in overcoming the difficult work I took on.

However, in order to improve my career prospects, professional growth, and employment opportunities, I began learning online skills. From virtual assistant to email copywriter to front-end developer, I've done it all.

I am a spirited and strong-willed woman who is focused on achieving her goals in life. I even hired a well-known copywriting guru to teach me how to write email copy because I want to improve my email copywriting skills for my career and potential clients.

I currently specialize in email marketing and copywriting, along with web design and development. My top priority is to use my creative, technical, and marketing skills to help entrepreneurs in reaching their business goals.

I would love to contribute to your and your company's success. So, if you think we'd be a good fit, send me an email and we'll get to know each other:)
